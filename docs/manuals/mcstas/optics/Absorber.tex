\subsection*{Description}
\begin{lstlisting}
Infinitely absorbing slab of material.

Example: Absorber(xmin=-0.01, xmax=0.01, ymin=-0.05, ymax=0.05, zmin=-0.2, zmax=0)
\end{lstlisting}
